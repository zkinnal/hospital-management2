# **Software Requirements Specification (SRS)**

## **Hospital Management System**

---

## **1. Introduction**

### **1.1 Purpose**

The purpose of this document is to describe the requirements for the Hospital Management System (HMS). The system will help manage hospital operations such as patient records, appointments, billing, staff management, and inventory.

### **1.2 Scope**

The HMS will:

* Automate hospital administrative processes
* Manage patient information and history
* Handle appointment scheduling
* Maintain billing and payment records
* Manage doctors, staff, and departments
* Track medicines and inventory

### **1.3 Definitions, Acronyms, Abbreviations**

* HMS – Hospital Management System
* OPD – Outpatient Department
* IPD – Inpatient Department
* EMR – Electronic Medical Record

---

## **2. Overall Description**

### **2.1 Product Perspective**

The HMS is a standalone or web-based application that integrates different hospital departments into a single system.

### **2.2 Product Functions**

* Patient registration and record management
* Appointment scheduling
* Doctor and staff management
* Billing and payment processing
* Pharmacy and inventory management
* Reporting and analytics

### **2.3 User Classes and Characteristics**

* **Admin**: Full access to system
* **Doctor**: View patients, update diagnosis
* **Receptionist**: Manage appointments and patient registration
* **Pharmacist**: Manage medicines and stock
* **Patient**: View records and appointments

### **2.4 Operating Environment**

* Web-based system (Chrome, Edge, etc.)
* Backend: Java/Python/.NET
* Database: MySQL/PostgreSQL
* OS: Windows/Linux

### **2.5 Design Constraints**

* Must ensure data security and privacy
* Should comply with healthcare standards
* User-friendly interface required

### **2.6 Assumptions and Dependencies**

* Users have basic computer knowledge
* Stable internet connection required
* Database server is available

---

## **3. System Features**

### **3.1 Patient Management**

**Description:** Manage patient records

**Functional Requirements:**

* Register new patient
* Update patient details
* View patient history
* Delete patient record (admin only)

---

### **3.2 Appointment Management**

**Description:** Schedule and manage appointments

**Functional Requirements:**

* Book appointment
* Cancel/reschedule appointment
* View doctor availability
* Send appointment reminders

---

### **3.3 Doctor Management**

**Description:** Manage doctor information

**Functional Requirements:**

* Add/update doctor profile
* Assign departments
* View schedules

---

### **3.4 Billing System**

**Description:** Handle patient billing

**Functional Requirements:**

* Generate bills
* Process payments (cash/card/online)
* Generate invoices
* Maintain billing history

---

### **3.5 Pharmacy Management**

**Description:** Manage medicines and stock

**Functional Requirements:**

* Add medicines
* Update stock
* Track expiry dates
* Generate purchase reports

---

### **3.6 User Management**

**Description:** Manage system users

**Functional Requirements:**

* Create user accounts
* Assign roles (admin, doctor, etc.)
* Authenticate users (login/logout)

---

### **3.7 Reporting Module**

**Description:** Generate reports

**Functional Requirements:**

* Daily/weekly/monthly reports
* Patient reports
* Financial reports

---

## **4. External Interface Requirements**

### **4.1 User Interface**

* Simple and intuitive UI
* Dashboard for quick access
* Forms for data entry

### **4.2 Hardware Interface**

* Computers, printers
* Barcode scanner (optional)

### **4.3 Software Interface**

* Database system (MySQL)
* Payment gateway integration (optional)

### **4.4 Communication Interface**

* Internet for web access
* SMS/email notifications

---

## **5. Non-Functional Requirements**

### **5.1 Performance Requirements**

* System should handle multiple users simultaneously
* Response time < 3 seconds

### **5.2 Security Requirements**

* User authentication and authorization
* Data encryption
* Secure patient data storage

### **5.3 Reliability**

* System uptime: 99%
* Regular backups

### **5.4 Usability**

* Easy to learn and operate
* Minimal training required

### **5.5 Scalability**

* System should support future expansion
